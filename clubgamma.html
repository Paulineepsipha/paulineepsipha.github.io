We are excited to announce that CHARUSAT University got the campus expert program and we are launching official student tech community named as Club Gamma.

 

What is the Campus Expert Program?
Campus Experts program is run by GitHub to enrich the technology communities on the campuses that are open to any student, ranging from newbie programmer to experts in industry and other academic disciplines.

Prerequisite to be in Club Gamma Team:
Non-Technical Skills: Leadership, Problem Solving, and Communication.
Technical Skills: Basic knowledge of programming

Perks of being in Club Gamma team:

As a Club Gamma Core Team, you will help a student community to grow with a goal of diversity and inclusion. You will be the pillar of the community in it's starting age. Together we will create an impact of Club Gamma - CHARUSAT to the world.

 

  • LEARN ✨
    You will get to learn hard and soft skills. You will enhance your social skills, managerial skills, leadership skills, writing skills etc.
  • BUILD 🔧This Club will provide you ample opportunities where you will be dealing with the real life projects, you will get a chance to develop a solution to problems of the real world. Obviously It will create great impact on your resume too.
  • MAKE CONNECTIONS 🤝
    You will get good time attending an event, seminar, webinar and workshops from the industry experts. You will get connected with the professionals of our field. You will organize the events, workshops, seminars etc too. It can be indeed life time experience and memories for you :P

 

You may get a recommendation to become a GitHub Campus Expert next year.
